About Me

Janyce Pickett is a graphic designer from Worcester, Massachusetts. She graduated from Worcester Technical High School with a certificate from Graphic Communications. She is currently attending the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth for a degree in Graphic Design. Some of her interests include design, video games, music, and pin collecting. She has always been interested in art and music and started to learning about careers in art in 6th grade. Her interest of getting into art stems from the web show RWBY and how she felt watching it. Making others feel good when they see her designs is one of her main goals for her career. Resume
